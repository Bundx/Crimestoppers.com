Emerging Crimes:The Case of Robberies in Kenya Report,2017

The crime of robberies with violence has been identified to be rapidly establishing itself around the country.The Global Robberies Review ,rates Kenya as number 17 out of 19 countries on High threat in Robberies with violence.The rising icidents of the crime of Robbery in kenya have raised serious security concerns among citizens in general and security agents in particular.

The most prevalent cases of robberies is where teenagers engage themselves in violent gang robberies handling crude weapons likes guns and knives and even engaging in retaliation against the police which has left many of our youths dead and even the police who sometimes die in thier line of duty.The public too has not been spared as innocent civillians are sometimes caught between the cross fires of the police task force and the thieves and some succumb to victims of violent robberies.
