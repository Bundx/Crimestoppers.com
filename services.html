Services

  • Overview

    Community crime prevention programs or strategies target changes in community infastructure ,culture or the physical enviroment in order to reduce crime.The diversity of approaches include neighbourhood watch,community policing through initiatives such as 'nyumba kumi' initiative,urban or physical design,and comprehensive or multi-displinary efforts.These strategies may seek to engage residents,communitynand faith based organisations and local government agiencies in addressing the factors that contibute to the community's crime,deliquency and disorder.

  • Programs

    Programs
    Title Topic Summary
    Adolescent Community Reinforcement Approach Drugs and substance Abuse An Outpatient program targeting 13 to 25 year olds that aims to replace activities supporting alcohol and drug use with positive behaviours that support recovery.The program is rated effective.Participants were more likely to seek out and continue care services,abstain from substance use (in particular,marijuana had less reported depression and internalised behaviours and more socially stable.)
    Adolescent Diversion Project Courts/Crime Prevention/Juveniles A strengths-based advocacy oriented program that diverts arrested youth from formal processing in the juvenile justice system and provides them community based services.This program is rated effective.The program is associated with a significant reduction in the rates of official deliquency of participating juveniles as compared to juveniles formally processed in the system.
    Adults in the Making(AIM) Crime Preventions/Juveniles A family centered preventive intervention designed to enhance the family process and self-regulatory competence to deter escalation of alcohol use and development of substance use problems.The program is rated effective.Overall,the predesponderance of evidence indicates that the program has positive impact on deterring the use of alcohol,drugs and involvement in other risky behaviours among participants.
    Dropout Prevention Programs Education-Attendance/Truancy School or community based programs targeting frequently absent students at risk of droping out of school.These programs are aimed at increasing school engagement,school attachment and academic performance of students with the main objective of increasing graduation rates and decreasing crime rates in the streets.
    Hot Spots Policing Crime and Deliquency Hot spot policing straregies focus on small geographic areas or places,usually in urban settings where crime is concentrated.The practise is rated effective.The analysis suggests that hotspot policing efforts that rely on problem-oriented policing strategies generate larger crime reduction effects than those that apply traditional policing strategies in crime hotspots.
